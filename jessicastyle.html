    ABOUT

    Detroit native, Candice ”Pretty Stringz” Smith, began playing the violin at nine years old. She graduated from the Detroit School of Arts, where other award-winning artist like Aaliyah have attended. While at the Detroit School of Arts, she served as the Assistant Concert Mistress for one year, followed by two years as Concert Mistress of the Advanced Orchestra.

     

    Recognized as a versatile artist, Candice plays: gospel, contemporary-Christian, smooth jazz, pop and R&B. Her well-rounded talent has landed her gigs opening for artist such as Vickie Winans and Day 26. Furthermore, she played in the Restored Me movie soundtrack (2016) and the Matthew Barney Film: River of Fundament (2015).

     

    Candice continued her education at Florida A&M University and graduated with a Bachelors degree in Music. In August 2017, she earned a Masters of Social Sciences with a concentration in Public Administration. Candice is a nationally renowned violinist, a philanthropist in support of the youth and a founder of an after school program for the performing arts’ which she plans to expand into a performing arts high school in the future.
